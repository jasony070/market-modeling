Background

Finding a model that predicts the markets has been long sought after by financial analysts, economic researchers, and traders. Computer scientists have more recently entered the race due to advances in machine learning, notably recurrent neural networks. Our project focuses on utilizing some of these advances to create a web app that predicts how the market will go in some period of time.
