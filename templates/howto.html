    • Get Started

    Step 1: Input the number of days you would like to predict.

    Step 2: Click on the parameters you would like to predict.

    Step 3: Switch between the two types of graphs.

    Parameters

    S&P500 One of the four major US indexes. It is a basket of 500 of the largest US stocks, weighted by market capitalization. Moreover, this index is widely considered to be the best indicator of how large US stocks perform on a daily basis. This index is maintained by S&P Dow Jones Indices, whcih is a join venture majority owned by S&P Global.

    USDJPY US Dollar to Japanese Yen exchange rate.

    GBPUSD British Pound to US Dollar exchange rate.

    AUDUSD Australian Dollar to US Dollar exchange rate.

    2 Year Treasury Yield This refers to the return on an investment in a 2 year US government debt obligation such as a bill, note, or bond. It is essentially the interest rate the government pays in order to borrow money for different periods of time.

    10 Year Treasury Yield This refers to the return on an investment in a 10 year US government debt obligation such as a bill, note, or bond. It is essentially the interest rate the government pays in order to borrow money for different periods of time.

    Gold Price of gold. This is typically correlated with how risky the investors think the other assets are.

    Oil Price of oil. This affects many industries that use a lot of oil for production.

    Graph Types

    Growth Rate -> The relative growth increase, expressed in units of time. Percentage change of a specific variable within a specific time period.

    Levels -> The actual values over a certain period of time.

    Input

    Input -> The amount of days the user wishes to predict the parameter into the future.
